Lasagna Recipe

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Tomato sauce
  • Sausage
  • Cottage cheese
  • Mozzarella
  • Parmesan
  • Cheddar

Steps

  1. Cook sausage until browned.
  2. Remove grease from sausage.
  3. Mix sausage with tomato sauce.
  4. Place a layer of lasagna noodles in a 9" by 11" pan.
  5. Coat noodles in 1/2 of the sausage and tomato sauce mixture.
  6. Sprinkle cottage cheese in the pan.
  7. Sprinkle parmesan in the pan.
  8. Sprinkle mozzarella in the pan.
  9. Add another layer of noodles to the pan.
  10. Add the rest of the sausage and tomato sauce.
  11. Sprinkle cottage cheese in the pan.
  12. Sprinkle parmesan in the pan.
  13. Sprinkle mozzarella in the pan.
  14. Sprinkle cheddar in the pan.
  15. Cover pan with tin foil.
  16. Preheat oven to 375 and cook for 1 hour.
