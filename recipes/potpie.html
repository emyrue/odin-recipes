Chicken Pot Pie Recipe

Ingredients

  • Boneless chicken breast
  • Cream of chicken soup
  • Mixed vegetables
  • Pie crust

Steps

  1. Boil and dice the chicken breasts.
  2. Mix chicken, chicken soup, and vegetables.
  3. Pour mixture into pie crust and cover with the second crust.
  4. Bake at 375 degrees for 45 minutes.
